Meet Rasheda

Rasheda Mills is a fintech founder and credit strategist with more than sixteen years of entrepreneurial experience.

As the founder of Credit Remedi she developed systems designed to help consumers repair their credit and position themselves for real funding opportunities.

She is also the creator of Meet Ally, an AI powered credit assistant built to simplify credit education, dispute strategies, and financial literacy.

Through her ecosystem of services, software, and education platforms, Rasheda helps individuals improve their credit profiles while also teaching entrepreneurs how to build profitable credit businesses.
