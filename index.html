Meet Rasheda

Rasheda Mills is a financial strategy specialist and business consultant with more than sixteen years of entrepreneurial experience.

As a business consultant, she developed systems designed to help consumers improve their financial health and position themselves for real business opportunities.

She is also the creator of Meet Ally, an AI powered financial literacy assistant built to simplify financial education, business strategies, and financial literacy.

Through her ecosystem of services, software, and education platforms, Rasheda helps individuals improve their financial health while also teaching entrepreneurs how to build profitable consulting businesses.
